Panel or popup?

Popup works just like you'd imagine it to work. Panel acts more like normal app window, also if panel is attached to the bottom of the screen it will show 'always-on-top'.

The catch is that you have to enable panels either via chrome://flags/ or --enable-panels

I know this isn't ideal, but popup windows don't support alwaysOnTop and this is only way to obtain it.
